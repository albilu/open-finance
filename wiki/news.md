---

## Overview

The Financial News widget displays curated headlines from major financial news sources directly inside Open-Finance. News feeds are **locale-aware** — English users see English-language sources, French users see French-language sources.

---

## Where to Find News

The news feed appears as a widget on the Dashboard and on a dedicated **News** page. Headlines are refreshed automatically every 30 minutes from the source feeds.

---

## Privacy

News requests are made server-side. The news sources see the server’s IP address, not yours. No financial data (account names, transactions, balances) is ever sent to any news source.
